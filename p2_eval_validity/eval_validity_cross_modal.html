The five recurring threats

the same bugs appear in every field — that repetition is the contribution

threat 1

Leakage & contamination

Train/test overlap inflates every field: MMLU leakage, benchmark memorization, WalkIndia clip-adjacency.

threat 2

Construct invalidity

The metric doesn't measure the capability — FVD ≠ driving, task-success ≠ reasoning.

threat 3

Saturation / Goodhart

Static suites overfit; progress decouples from competence as the benchmark is optimized.

threat 4

Judge fragility & reward hacking

LLM-as-judge is gamed; RL agents exploit rewards; both inflate scores without real gains.

threat 5

Sim2real / fidelity-utility gap

Looks right, acts wrong — sim doesn't transfer, prediction quality ≠ downstream behavior.
